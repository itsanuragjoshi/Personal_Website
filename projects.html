SeamsUnbranded

Problem/Brief
To create a website for customers to converse, ideate and shop with purpose, for a purpose i.e., to become part of the movement towards making conscious, sustainable, purposeful choices.

Customer/User

  • Purposeful “woke” shoppers who are investing in sustainability
  • Cultivating an ethical wardrobe – eco-friendly closet with upcycled labels
  • Conscious citizens who care about social and environmental justice

Key Features

  • Thinking Den – where users who got an environmental concern / thought / fashion design that promote sustainability could test their idea / designs
  • Custom ecommerce website with CMS for content updation, coded ground-up, required all necessary features / functionality including product management, inventory management (SKU level), customer management and order management (cancelled, returns, replacement, delivery)

Tech/Tools

  • Site mapping – GlooMaps
  • Wireframing – Figma
  • Design – Adobe Photoshop
  • Development – HTML / CSS3 / JS (Front-end), PHP (Back-end)

Brand/Client
NewMe

Problem/Brief
To create an online ultra-affordable fast-fashion ecommerce store featuring styles for women and men. It aims to offer 1000+ new exclusive designs per week in limited stocks via ultra-convenient and personalized shopping experience for GenZs/GenAs

Customer/User

  • Gen Zs and Gen As who use social media to seek inspiration, research products and connect with their favorite brands. Influencers account for a large part of the purchasing decision
  • Demands sustainable shopping and embraces mobile apps & payments
  • Makes purchases to stay relevant and lift their mood
  • Desire for reasonable prices with an interest in high-quality products

Key Features

  • A clean, minimalist, grid-based PWA website layout to ensure a smooth, ultra-convenient UX in the shoppers’ journey from discovery → purchase → post-purchase
  • Using shoppers’ information (body measurements) to recommend and help them pick the right size for the right style. Also providing accurate sizing information on product detail page to curb return rate due to sizing issue
  • Automating product assortment and sort order decisions: Present each shopper with the most relevant selection of products, ordered in an optimum way to maximise sales
  • Identifying micro-trends driven by social media to influence everything from search and recommendations to promotional panels, to instantly push trending products to the foreground

Tech/Tools

  • Site mapping – GlooMaps
  • Wireframing – Figma
  • Design – Adobe Photoshop
  • Development – React (Front-end), Shopify (Back-end)

Brand/Client
WeBeeSocial

Problem/Brief
To create a super easy-to-use accounting SaaS platform which help startups and small businesses save time by managing payables and receivables, with easy reconciliation along with statement and reports generation.

Customer/User

  • Internal Teams - Account Management/Client Servicing, Finance, Admin, CXOs
  • External (future) – SMBs, Startups etc.

Key Features

  • Manage Payables – customers, estimates, invoices, sales orders, credit notes, payment received, customer statement
  • Manage Receivables – vendors, expenses, purchase orders, debit notes, payment made, vendor statement
  • Create templates for payables and receivables, manage currencies & taxes
  • Generate reports – business overview (profit and loss, balance sheet, cash flow statement), sales, payables, receivables, sales, purchases & expenses, taxes

Tech/Tools

  • Site mapping – GlooMaps
  • Wireframing – Figma
  • Design – Adobe Photoshop
  • Development – React (Front-end), PHP (Back-end)
